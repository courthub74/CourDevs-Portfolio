About Me:

My name is Courtney Jacobs. I am a solutions inclined, analytical, and creative Full Stack Web Developer, possesing experience in design, development, testing, and implementation of web application software.

{% comment %} I have had a fascination with coding since my first internship, studying computer science in college. I was a support technician for a Software as a service company.
