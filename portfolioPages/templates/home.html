About Me:

I have had a fascination with coding since my first internship, studying computer science in college. I was a support technician for a Software as a service company. What started as a mere one day a week gig turned into a 7 year long work experience, which later elevated to team supervisor as the company grew. Part of my responsibility was to test and debug software. I gained much experience with the SDLC (Software Development Life-Cycle) and fell in love with the process of building an application with syntax ever since. Web Application development is something I would pursue as a hobby if money was no object. Below you will find projects that I've completed to showcase what I've learned on my coding journey.
